About Glow Bar

Glow Bar is a premium Nepalese skincare brand dedicated to creating natural, handmade, and chemical-free soaps. Carefully crafted in Butwal, Nepal, our soaps are made using naturally available ingredients that are gentle on the skin and environmentally friendly.

What makes Glow Bar unique is its multi-purpose formula. A single bar can be used as a body soap, facial cleanser, and hair wash, making it a convenient and all-in-one natural solution for daily hygiene and skincare.

Each Glow Bar soap is enriched with skin-nourishing natural oils and botanical ingredients that help cleanse, moisturize, and refresh your skin while maintaining its natural balance. The mild formulation makes it suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin.

Our soaps are free from harsh chemicals, artificial preservatives, and synthetic additives, ensuring a pure and safe bathing experience. By combining traditional handmade techniques with the richness of Nepal’s natural resources, Glow Bar delivers a product that promotes healthy skin, natural beauty, and sustainable living.

At Glow Bar, we believe that true glow comes from nature.

FAQs

Is Glow Bar suitable for all skin types?

Yes. The formulation is mild and designed for all skin types, including sensitive skin.

Can I use it as shampoo?

Yes. Glow Bar is multipurpose. Hair types vary, so feel free to follow with a light rinse/conditioner if desired.

Does it contain harsh chemicals?

No. Our soaps are free from harsh chemicals, artificial preservatives, and synthetic additives.

How should I store my bar?

Keep your bar dry between uses (on a draining soap dish) to extend its life.
