Natural Ingredients We Use

We source the finest natural ingredients from Nepal's rich biodiversity. Every component is carefully selected for its skin-loving properties.

🍯

Raw Honey

Natural humectant that locks in moisture, soothes inflammation, and promotes a radiant glow with antibacterial properties.

🥥

Coconut Oil

Rich in fatty acids, deeply moisturizes and repairs skin barrier. Creates a gentle, luxurious lather for effective cleansing.

🌿

Aloe Vera

Cooling and hydrating gel that calms irritation, accelerates healing, and provides deep nourishment to every skin layer.

🌾

Turmeric (Haldi)

Essential Oils

Lavender, tea tree, and other therapeutic essential oils for natural fragrance, aromatherapy, and skin healing.

🌰

Shea Butter

Ultra-moisturizing butter rich in vitamins A and E. Protects skin elasticity and provides lasting hydration.

Neem Extract

Powerful antibacterial and antifungal properties that combat acne, blemishes, and skin infections naturally.

Coffee Grounds

Natural exfoliant that boosts circulation, reduces cellulite appearance, and gives skin a smooth, energized texture.
