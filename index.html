About Glow Bar

Glow Bar is a premium Nepalese skincare brand dedicated to creating natural, handmade, and chemical-free soaps. Carefully crafted in Butwal, Nepal, our soaps are made using naturally available ingredients that are gentle on the skin and environmentally friendly.

What makes Glow Bar unique is its multi-purpose formula. A single bar can be used as a body soap, facial cleanser, and hair wash, making it a convenient and all-in-one natural solution for daily hygiene and skincare.

Each Glow Bar soap is enriched with skin-nourishing natural oils and botanical ingredients that help cleanse, moisturize, and refresh your skin while maintaining its natural balance.

❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Glow Bar.

Is Glow Bar suitable for sensitive skin?
+

Yes! Glow Bar's mild formulation is designed for all skin types, including sensitive skin. Our soaps are free from harsh chemicals and synthetic additives that can irritate sensitive skin.

Can I really use it as shampoo?
+

Absolutely! Glow Bar is a true multipurpose bar. You can use it as body soap, facial cleanser, and hair wash. The natural oils help cleanse without stripping your hair's natural moisture.

What ingredients do you use?
+

We use naturally available ingredients like coconut oil, olive oil, essential oils, honey, turmeric, aloe vera, activated charcoal, and other botanical extracts. Zero harsh chemicals or synthetic additives.

How long does one bar last?
+

With regular daily use, a single Glow Bar typically lasts 4-6 weeks. To extend its life, store it on a dry soap dish between uses and keep it away from standing water.

Do you deliver across Nepal?
+

Yes! We deliver across Nepal. For orders and delivery inquiries, please DM us on our Instagram page @glowbar06 or use the contact form below.

Are your soaps cruelty-free?
+

Yes! Glow Bar products are never tested on animals. We are committed to cruelty-free and eco-friendly practices in everything we do.
