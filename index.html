Our Story

Born in the Heart of Nepal, Crafted for the World

Glow Bar was born in Butwal, Nepal with a simple mission — to create the purest, most natural soaps using the incredible ingredients gifted by the Himalayas. Every bar is handcrafted using traditional cold-process methods, ensuring all the natural goodness is preserved.

Our soaps are designed to be multi-purpose — perfect for your face, body, and even hair. No chemicals, no synthetic fragrances, no toxins. Just pure, clean, honest soap that works beautifully on all skin types.
