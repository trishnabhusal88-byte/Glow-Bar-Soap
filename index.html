Our Story

Crafted with Love in the Heart of Nepal

"True glow comes from nature"

Glow Bar is a premium Nepalese skincare brand dedicated to creating natural, handmade, and chemical-free soaps. Carefully crafted in Butwal, Nepal, our soaps are made using naturally available ingredients that are gentle on the skin and environmentally friendly.

By combining traditional handmade techniques with the richness of Nepal's natural resources, Glow Bar delivers a product that promotes healthy skin, natural beauty, and sustainable living.

Pure Ingredients

Nature's Finest, In Every Bar

We carefully select each ingredient from Nepal's natural bounty to create soaps that truly nurture your skin.

Coconut Oil

Rich in fatty acids, coconut oil provides deep moisturization, creates a luxurious lather, and protects the skin's natural barrier.

Turmeric

A powerful antioxidant with anti-inflammatory properties. Turmeric brightens skin, reduces blemishes, and promotes an even skin tone.

Aloe Vera

Known for its soothing and healing properties, aloe vera hydrates the skin, reduces irritation, and promotes cell regeneration.

Essential Oils

Therapeutic-grade essential oils like lavender, tea tree, and rosemary provide aromatherapy benefits and natural fragrance.

Raw Honey

A natural humectant that locks in moisture, raw honey also has antibacterial properties that keep skin clear and healthy.

Shea Butter

Extremely nourishing and rich in vitamins A and E, shea butter deeply conditions the skin and provides lasting moisture.
