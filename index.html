About Us

Handcrafted With Love From the Heart of Nepal

Glow Bar is a premium Nepalese handmade and homemade soap brand dedicated to creating natural, chemical-free soaps. Carefully crafted in Butwal, Nepal, our soaps are made using naturally available ingredients that are gentle on the skin and environmentally friendly.
