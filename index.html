Our Story

Born in Nepal, Crafted for the World

Glow Bar was born in Butwal, Nepal — to create the purest soaps using Himalayan ingredients. Every bar is handcrafted using traditional cold-process methods, preserving all natural goodness.

Our soaps are multi-purpose — perfect for face, body, and hair. No chemicals, no synthetic fragrances, no toxins. Just pure, honest soap for all skin types.
